Executing your plan to achieve your goals.

Your Distance From Your Goal:

You’ve done a wonderful job of turning your goals into action. We suggest a few additional steps to ensure that your actions remain aligned with your goals.

Key Action Steps

  • Review your important goals at least annually. Maintaining clarity on what you want to achieve and why you want to achieve it can prove motivating.
  • Don’t forget the “why,” because when you start to veer off track (and you probably will), the “why” pulls you back to center—and what you really want becomes reality.

These actions can propel you toward your goals even more rapidly.
