Good news: The emergency fund you’ve created represents an important first step in securing your financial future. Your answers indicate, though, that you would benefit from building a stronger safety net.

It’s time to focus on your goals—and achieving those goals will require you to identify opportunities to reduce your spending or increase your earning. 

Key Action Steps

  • We recommend that you continue or begin using a cash flow tracker to help with this. That tool will reveal areas in which you may spend more than you recognize—which means you’ve discovered opportunities for improvement.
  • Some of these opportunities may represent “easy wins,” such as cutting down on eating out or canceling monthly subscriptions you no longer need or want. Remember, you're in charge. No one is telling you what you must cut or change.

Once you see the full picture, you will find it much easier to make informed choices to help you move more rapidly toward what you really want in life.
