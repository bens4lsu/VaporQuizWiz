Key Action Steps

Whatever your reason for completing Your Expansion Passport®, we suggest that you may have enough for what you need, just not everything you want.

  • Begin by focusing on smart budgeting. That may seem overwhelming or mysterious, but it doesn’t have to be.
  • The first step is to use a cash flow tracker (you can find several free ones online) to help you cut spending and start saving.
  • Some of the opportunities it reveals may represent “easy wins,” such as cutting down on eating out or canceling monthly subscriptions you no longer need or want. Remember, you're in charge. No one is telling you what you must cut or change.
  • This may also be a good time to look at your current career and/or income to see if there are ways to earn additional income (such as asking for a raise, looking at a different opportunity, or adding another form of income).

Once you see the full picture, you will likely find it much easier to make informed choices to help you move more rapidly toward what you really want in your life.
