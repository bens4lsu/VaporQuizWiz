Foundation Mindset

Protecting yourself and those who are important to you.

Your Distance From Your Goal:

You’ve done a good job of putting in place some of the key protections that establish a safety net for you and your loved ones. Now it’s time to take the next steps.

Key Action Steps

  • The steps to clarifying your goals and reassessing your current position involve (a) calculating your need and (b) defining your risk.
  • Calculating the need means coming up with a number that represents how much you and/or your loved ones would need if you were no longer able to work.
  • Calculating the risk means measuring the difference between your total needs and the savings and protections (insurance) you currently have in place.
  • To do that, use an insurance calculator or speak with a financial advisor.
  • In addition to measuring your need and risk, don’t forget to also ensure you have wills, trusts, powers of attorney, etc. in place to protect the ones you love the most.

Taking these steps can do a great deal to provide peace of mind to you and your loved ones if the unexpected happens.
