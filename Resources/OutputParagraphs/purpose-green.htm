Purpose Mindset

Your plan to guide your “walkaway” future.

Your Distance From Your Goal:

You've done a great job of clarifying your vision and plan for your “next chapter,” once you "walkaway" from your job or business. Articulating your goals for your next chapter clearly and making concrete plans to achieve them can help to ensure that it proves fulfilling for you.

As our lives change, sometimes our "walkaway" purpose changes. Reviewing your plan at least annually helps to ensure that your plan remains relevant. Make sure that your plans and strategies include considering the psychological aspects of retirement; how to stay connected socially; and how to handle health care costs, Medicare, and Social Security.
