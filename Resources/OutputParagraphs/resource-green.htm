Resource Mindset

Gain the insight to know not just how much you make but also how much you keep.

Your Distance From Your Goal:

By tracking your cash flow carefully, you’ve taken a critically important step in ensuring that you stay on the right financial path.

Key Action Steps

  • If you haven’t done so already, we suggest you schedule a recurring event in your calendar each month that designates the date and time that you will review that month’s income and expenses, just to be sure that you continue your habit of monitoring these data carefully.
  • Now may also be a good time to allocate extra revenue to your long-term goals.
  • Consider setting up separate tracking and/or bank accounts for the goals you want to achieve, such as taking vacations, funding college, or purchasing or remodeling a house.

Doing so will let you continue moving toward your goals, the kind of life you really want.
