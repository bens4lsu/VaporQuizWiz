Wellness Mindset

Enhancing your health and wellness so you can enjoy your life and live it fully.

Your Distance From Your Goal:

Congratulations on taking concrete steps for a healthy future! To continue your progress, begin by clarifying your goals for this mindset—what’s truly most important to you?

  • Do you want to increase the odds that you can live and work healthily beyond conventional expectations?
  • Do you want to maintain a healthy weight, enjoy more energy, or be fit enough to enjoy particular activities?

Gaining clarity on why you want to improve your health or fitness can prove motivating.

Key Action Steps

Once you know where you’re heading and why, take steps to increase your chances of success:

  • Develop both support and accountability, perhaps by hiring a personal trainer or enlisting the help of a friend.
  • Because nutrition comprises the biggest piece of our fitness quest, we suggest that you make it fun to learn about different nutrition “hacks” and set a goal of eating healthily 80% of the time—none of us is perfect! Consider tracking what you eat, either in a journal or via an online tracker or app.
  • Do the same with your activity. Whether you use a fitness tracker, journal, or something else, track your progress. Incorporating apps, devices, and personal trainers to make it fun to exercise and eat healthily.
  • Don't forget that wellness includes a mental as well as a physical component. Think about whether you need to manage stress more effectively--particularly if your family, career, or business places high demands on you. The American Institute of Stress states that over 80% of workers report experiencing work-related stress. If you're one of them, consider incorporating mindfulness and/or meditation techniques. Ensure you seek support from friends and/or family and focus on positive coping strategies.

Taking these actions can put you on a path to better wellness.
