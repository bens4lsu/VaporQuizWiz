Longevity Mindset

Ensuring you will be cared for as you wish, should the need arise.

Your Distance From Your Goal:

You have done a good job of planning for long-term care. You can take pride in this accomplishment: As healthcare costs rise, ensuring that you can provide long-term care becomes increasingly important.

Key Action Steps

  • Because these rising costs change your risk profile, review your long-term care protection at least annually. Reassess your risk to ensure that you maintain appropriate coverage.
  • This may also be a good time to share your long-term care wishes with a loved one.

Taking these additional steps now will ensure continued peace of mind for you and your loved ones.
