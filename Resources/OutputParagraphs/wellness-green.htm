Key Action Steps

Continue to look for ways to increase your knowledge about and practice of healthy habits:

  • Because nutrition comprises the biggest piece of our fitness quest, we suggest that you make it fun to learn about different nutrition “hacks” and set a goal of eating healthily 80% of the time.
  • Focus also on making exercise a fundamental part of who you truly are, so it becomes a lifelong habit. Consider incorporating apps or devices that can not only keep you focused but also inject more fun into your healthy lifestyle.
  • Make sure that healthy eating and exercise become lifelong habits.
  • Don't forget that wellness includes a mental as well as a physical component. Think about whether you need to manage stress more effectively--particularly if your family, career, or business places high demands on you. The American Institute of Stress states that over 80% of workers report experiencing job-related stress. If you're among them, consider incorporating mindfulness and/or meditation techniques (apps can help), ensuring that you seek social support from friends and/or family, and focus on positive coping strategies. Doing these things can help ensure that you avoid stress-related illnesses.

Adding these actions can ensure that you remain on a path to lifelong wellness.
