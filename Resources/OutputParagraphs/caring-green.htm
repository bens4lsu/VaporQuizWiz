Caring Mindset

Sharing key information with your loved ones.

Your Distance From Your Goal:

You’ve taken critical steps to protect your loved ones in case you were out of commission. We suggest that you ensure that your system remains up to date.

Key Action Steps

To ensure your loved ones will have what they need, even if conditions change:

  • Add assets, liabilities, and key documents to the binder or vault in which your information is compiled as soon as they’re acquired and ensure that you review and update the information at least annually, to keep it current.
  • This may also be a good time to review all of the documents with a professional financial advisor to confirm that everything you need is in place and up to date.

This small investment ensures that your loved ones will remain protected.
