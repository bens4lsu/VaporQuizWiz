Key Action Steps

Here are some important actions to help you create a productive partnership:

  • One big preparation for forming this partnership will be to clarify your goals by identifying and writing down what you really want in your life.
  • You might also benefit from considering why you haven't yet worked with a financial advisor.
  • A good place to start would be to consider whether this is really a “why” or a “how” issue: In other words, do you question why you should work with a financial advisor (whether you need one), or is the issue more a question of not knowing how to work with a financial advisor or how to find one who will be a good fit for you?
